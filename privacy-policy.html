Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2026

What we collect

Nothing. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels. The only data stored is a local authentication token in your browser's localStorage to keep you signed in — this never leaves your device.

Cookies

We do not use cookies.

Third parties

We do not share data with any third party. No advertising networks, no analytics providers.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to know what data we hold about you. We hold none. To clear your local sign-in state, clear your browser's localStorage or use your browser's "Clear site data" option.

Contact

For any privacy questions email privacy@econos.co.uk.
